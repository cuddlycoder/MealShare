WE ARE ENDING HUNGER IN OUR COMMUNITY

World hunger affects millions globally, with 149 million children under five stunted and 45 million wasted due to malnutrition in 2020. The COVID-19 pandemic worsened food insecurity, adding an estimated 100 million people to those facing hunger.

MealShare is an application that allows people to make a difference. From delivery people to donators, a member of the community will take the role.
